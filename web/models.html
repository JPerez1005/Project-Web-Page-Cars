Audi urbansphere concept


In the technical section, Audi offers a 100% electric model with two motors with four-wheel drive. Together, both engines develop an approximate power of 396 HP and 690 Nm. These figures are accompanied by a 120 kWh capacity battery (10 kWh more than the BMW iX) that allows you to travel up to 750 kilometers on a charge, according to the WLTP cycle. To improve its autonomy, the Urbansphere is capable of deactivating the front engine as a way to take advantage of the vehicle's inertia. Charging times are short, being able to recharge at 270 kW and go from 5 to 80% in 25 minutes. The Urbansphere is completed with adaptive air suspensions and rear wheel steering.



Lamborghini Aventator LP


The exterior of the Aventador LP700-4 is influenced in its lines by the Lamborghini Reventon, an exclusive concept from the Italian firm presented in 2007, of which only 20 units were manufactured. The Sant'Agata supercar is 4.78 meters long, 1.14 meters high and a striking 2.26 meters wide, achieved thanks to a generous rear track width. The doors open in a scissor shape, in the same way as the rest of the V12 models since the time of the Lamborghini Countach. At the rear, an imposing diffuser and a striking hexagonal exhaust outlet stand out. Lamborghini offers a palette of thirteen shades for the body of the Aventador LP700-4.



Mercedes AMG-Gt


Under the hood, we already know that it will once again have the 4.0-liter turbocharged V8 engines of the outgoing generation, which has been improved with a new oil pan, repositioned intercooler and active ventilation and above all a higher boost pressure, a greater airflow and software settings. As a result, as is the case in the SL, with these engines the 55 4MATIC+ version offers a power of 476 HP and 700 Nm, and the GT 63 4MATIC+ 585 HP and 800 Nm, both as already noted in their last name accompanied by a 4MATIC+ all-wheel drive as the main novelty together with an AMG Speedshift MCT 9G transmission with wet start clutch that allows for shorter shift times, to offer 0 to 100 in just 3.9 seconds and 3.2 seconds respectively, while the Maximum speed reaches 295 km/h and 315 km/h.



Porshe Mission X


It is a concept and there is no power or performance data, but there is data on its charging system, at 900 volts and twice as fast as the Taycan Turbo S. The batteries are centered behind the seats to achieve the best weight distribution and achieve a perfect dynamic balance. Its ultralight construction makes the most of the use of carbon fiber that creates a structure around the two occupants that Porsche calls an exoskeleton. The silver color of its predecessors gives way to an elegant metallic ocher called Rocket Metallic, in the trend of so-called organic colors that are beginning to be in fashion.



McLaren 765LT Spider


The main protagonist of this Spider variant is the retractable rigid roof that it equips, made in a single piece and capable of opening or closing in just 11 seconds and on the go, as long as we do not exceed 50 km/h. Without a doubt, this speed is one of the qualities that most distinguishes this roof and the other is its lightness, because despite the electrohydraulic system that it includes, it only adds 49 kilos to the whole, making this McLaren 765LT Spider a real ride. sporty, without special reinforcements for its frame which – remember – is made of carbon fiber.



MCLaren Artura


The main innovation of the Artura is its plug-in hybrid propulsion scheme. The main engine is a 3.0 V6 Twin-Turbo gasoline engine with 585 HP of maximum power at 7,500 rpm and a maximum torque of 585 Nm between 2,250 and 7,000 rpm. This engine is called M630 and its main characteristic is the use of a 120º V, installing the two turbochargers inside the V. It is a very unusual design due to the angle between cylinder banks, but McLaren defends it in order to offer a very low center of gravity.
